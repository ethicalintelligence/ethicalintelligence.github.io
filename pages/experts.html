THE EI NETWORK

Ethical Intelligence (EI) was founded June 2019 in Edinburgh, Scotland by Anna van Oosterzee and Olivia Gambelin. They met at the University of Edinburgh where both were completing their Masters degrees in Philosophy; van Oosterzee in Mind, Language, and Embodied Cognition and Gambelin in Ethics of AI. The two were on the founding committee of the Beneficial AI Society, during which time they discovered a significant market demand for ethics in technology alongside a strong supply of young minds looking to pursue careers in this field. It was here that the idea for Ethical Intelligence was born.
