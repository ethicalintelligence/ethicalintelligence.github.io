Ethics Training

A comprehensive immersion into Tech and AI Ethics, "Putting Ethics into Action" is designed specifically to establish an ethical literacy in technology backed by an in-depth understanding of contemporary research. The course runs for six weeks, by the end of which you will have both a baseline understanding of the field as well as the insight into how to apply this knowledge in your own business and tech practices.

The curriculum includes:

  • Introduction to AI Ethics and its philosophical foundations
  • Fairness and Bias
  • Privacy and Security
  • Sustainability
  • Transparency, accountability and Explainability
  • Current state of guidelines and laws relevant to AI ethics

Ethics Risk Audit

An objective, independent examination of current technologically focused practices conducted by trained experts to mitigate risk of ethical scandals and boost consumer trust. The EI Ethics Risk Audit brings transparency to technical development and usage by fighting against ethics washing, as well as checks practices against current international policies to ensure responsible innovation.

Ethical areas of risk include:

  • Human dignity
  • Autonomy
  • Responsibility
  • Justice, equity and solidarity
  • Democracy
  • Accountability and Explainability
  • Security, safety, bodily and mental integrity
  • Data protection and privacy
  • Sustainability

Custom Ethics Protocols

Integrating company values with current internationally recognized policy guidelines, EI Protocols are bespoke tools developed in collaboration with medium to large organizations, empowering them with the ability to consistently check the ethics of their own and their partners’ technology against a core ethical framework.

Includes:

  • Hand-selected interdisciplinary expert teams led by EI’s professional management
  • Translation of company’s values into workable ethical framework
  • In-depth analysis and application of current policy guidelines in tech and AI ethics
  • Custom Ethical Protocol designed to embed ethics into everyday tech practices
  • Operational guidance in adaptation of protocol into company processes
